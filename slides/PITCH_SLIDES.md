## Slide 1 — The SDLC inflection (2024–2026)

**Title:** Software development is shifting from assistance to agency

**Bullets:**

- Developers delegate **multi-file outcomes** (features, fixes, tests)—not just lines
- **Context** (repo + rules + docs) matters more than raw model IQ
- CTO mandate: **↑ velocity** and **↓ risk**—not one or the other

**Speaker note:** Tie to Responsive shipping AI-powered questionnaire workflows while staying SOC2-clean.

**Optional visual:**

```
Before:  Developer ↔ IDE ↔ PR

```
